OUR STORY

TECHMANIA was founded in 2017 with a vision to bring cutting-edge technology to Nepal at affordable prices. What started as a small tech shop in Kathmandu has now grown into one of the nation's most trusted technology brands.

OUR MISSION

We believe technology should be accessible, reliable, and empowering. Our mission is to bridge the gap between global tech innovations and the Nepalese market, ensuring our customers get premium products with local support.

OUR TEAM

Our team consists of passionate tech enthusiasts, engineers, and customer service professionals who are dedicated to providing the best products and support. We're proud to be 100% Nepali-operated while maintaining global standards.

QUALITY COMMITMENT

Every TECHMANIA product undergoes rigorous testing to meet international quality standards. We partner with leading global manufacturers while adding our own quality controls to ensure durability and performance in Nepalese conditions.
